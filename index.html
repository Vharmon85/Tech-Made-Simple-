What is AI? (It’s Not as Scary as You Think)

When I first heard about AI, my first thought was “this sounds really complicated.” It even reminded me of movies like Terminator, which made it feel a little intimidating.

But after learning more, I realized AI isn’t anything crazy — it’s just technology designed to help people.

AI is already everywhere. It helps recommend videos, answer questions, and make everyday tasks easier. Most people use AI every day without even realizing it.

The truth is, AI isn’t something to be afraid of. It’s just a tool, and like any tool, it depends on how people use it.
