What is AI? (It’s Not as Scary as You Think)

When I first heard about AI, I thought it sounded really complicated. It even reminded me of movies like Terminator, which made it feel a little intimidating at first.

That curiosity is what pushed me to learn more instead of just being confused or worried about it.

AI is simply technology that helps computers learn from data and make things easier for people. You already use it every day with things like YouTube recommendations or voice assistants.

At the end of the day, AI isn’t something to fear. It’s just a tool, and it depends on how people use it.
