What is AI?

AI is the future and it is here. AI is the future generation of technology. It is what you can call a smart helper that lives inside computers. Or is it?

When I first heard about AI, it sounded scary. There was a time I actually thought it was the Antichrist. I was very concerned about AI becoming such a big part of our daily lives. I was concerned about it because I remember what the AI and robots did in the Terminator movie. If you can recall, the robots and AI end up taking over, turning bad, and trying to wipe out the human race.

I am not saying that it is not possible we could end up at war with the computer systems that humans created. But only if it got into the wrong hands of people using it for evil things, to hurt people, or to scam people.

People think AI is so complicated, but it really isn't. AI learns at a basic level, just like humans do when starting out. AI looks at a lot of information in a short time and learns from it. They are able to look at a huge amount of information in a short period of time, gather all that information, and learn from it within a short time frame. Pretty impressive, I would say.

Think of it like this: the more examples it sees, the better it is at recognizing patterns in the information it is given. AI learns from seeing information over and over again and recognizes the patterns in the information it is given. AI doesn't really think on its own. It follows a process with its own steps and instructions to help make decisions.

AI as a Tool

AI is and can be a very useful tool for everyday life. It saves time by giving people suggestions on projects or a better way of doing things. It is also very helpful in the business aspect of things, helping with projects within business organizations and helping with workflows within the organizations. It helps with the hiring process and makes things a lot easier for companies hiring people.

Now there are ways that AI can be used that are not so good. Depending on how or who has built the AI and how they have trained it to be, it can be used to make fake posts on social media that could cause people to be misled in bad ways and cause them to be upset. It can be used to take people's money or scam people. Tracking personal data and privacy concerns—if not managed properly, AI could cause a huge problem.

AI itself is not a bad tool to utilize. It can be a problem based on how people try to use it. There are rules to using AI, and Artificial Intelligence itself follows strict safety rules and regulations because the ones building them made them for ethical use.

Final Thoughts

Like I said, AI sounded scary at first and intimidating to me. I felt like it was too complicated to use and understand. But after I started learning more about what it does and how it operates, it became very easy to understand how it operates and works. It is, after all, just technology that helps make our lives easier and faster.

The more I learn about AI, the less confusing it becomes. At the end of the day, AI isn't really something to be scared of. It is just a tool, and like any tool, it depends on how people use it. Understanding AI doesn't make it less powerful—it makes it less intimidating.
